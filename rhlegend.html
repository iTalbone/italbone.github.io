Relic Hunters Legend

Roles: Narrative Designer

Relic Hunters Legend is an online looter/shooter developed by Rogue Snail, to be released and published by Gearbox Publishing. In this project i worked mainly as Narrative Designer, having rewritten the backstory of one of the main character, as well as writing, designing and implementing a few full missions about this same character.

Relic Hunters Legend is not yet released, but you can check their updates on twitter.
